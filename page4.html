Manipulation

This is a tactic used by aggressors on the internet to subtly exploit the psychological states of victims. The most common users of this tactic are predators and scammers, as they seek to psychologically manipulate you into providing them with various materials, from your credit card details, to pictures of yourself. In short, if you ever encounter a stranger seeking to build a connection with you, it most likely isn’t something good. If you’re ever unsure, contact a parent or other trusted adult, and explain the problem to them.
